FAQ

How does the food builder work?
Our custom builder allows you to select your base, proteins, and toppings in real-time to create a meal tailored to your taste and nutritional needs.
Can I save my custom build?
Yes, registered users can save their favorite builds to their profile for easy re-ordering later.
Do you offer free delivery?
We offer free delivery for all orders over $30 within our primary delivery zones.
Is the food halal?
Absolutely. All our meat and poultry ingredients are strictly 100% certified halal.
