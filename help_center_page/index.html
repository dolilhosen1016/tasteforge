Popular Topics

How do I build my own meal?
Simply go to our Menu page, select your preferred base, and follow the step-by-step process to add proteins, toppings, and sauces. Real-time nutrition facts will update automatically!
How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery usually takes 30-45 minutes depending on your location and traffic conditions. You can track your order live from your dashboard.
Can I modify my order after placing?
You can modify or cancel your order within the first 5 minutes of placing it. After that, our chefs start preparing your meal immediately.
Do you offer subscriptions?
Yes! We offer weekly and monthly meal prep subscriptions. You can set your dietary preferences and let us handle your meals.
Is the food halal?
Absolutely. All our meat and poultry ingredients are strictly 100% certified halal.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2026

At TasteForge, your privacy is our priority. This policy outlines how we handle your personal data when you use our services, ensuring full compliance with global data protection standards.

1. Information We Collect

We collect essential data to provide a seamless ordering experience:

  • Account Details: Name, email, phone number, and delivery address.
  • Payment Data: Securely tokenized payment info (processed via Stripe/SSLCommerz).
  • Device Info: IP address, browser type, and interaction metrics.

2. How We Protect Your Data

We implement military-grade AES-256 encryption to protect your personal and financial information. We strictly do not sell, rent, or trade your data to third-party marketing agencies.

3. Cookies & Tracking

Cookies help us remember your cart items and preferred meals. You have full control and can disable non-essential cookies via your browser settings anytime.

4. User Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to access, modify, or completely delete your data from our servers. To request data deletion, please contact our Data Protection Officer.
